Prototype stage

One robot, three domains

MUR is a low-cost autonomous underwater vehicle (AUV) built to serve commercial, environmental, and safety applications from a single hardware platform.

Commercial

Underwater bridge and infrastructure inspection, 3D mapping of underwater structures and terrain.

Environmental

Monitoring underwater pollution levels and tracking environmental impact in real time.

Safety / Military

Underwater mine detection and related defense and security applications.
